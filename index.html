Basic Problems

Blind and visually impared have the absence of the following features in their lives

Independence

The people are dependent on some assistance to perform basic chores

Environment

The people have very low understanding of their environment

Accessibility

The people have very limited accessibility and face many problems in their lives

About Us



We are a group of energy driven enthusiasts that want to bring a change to the world. We try to do so by creating a pair of glasses for blind and visually impaired people that is not only help them in their daily lives but also enable them to see things for the first time in their lives. Currently we are developing the prototype which is expected to be released on May 2019
-->

Amazing Features.

Following are the amazing features of our glasses

Object and Hindrance Detection

For Detecting Objects and Avoiding Collisions

Scene Detection

Detect scenes and informs user of his/her surrounding. Helps in keeping track of the activity

Vehicle Detection

Detects the incoming vehicle and it's speed. Helps user in crossing the road

Optical Character Recognition

Read out aloud various text present

Self Learning

Automatic learning of various environment variables via various form factors

Currency Recognition

Recognition of fake and real currency bills in real time. Automatically count the bills

Facial Recognition

Automatic recognition of faces of various people a user will meet daily

Navigation

Guides the user in various indoor situations as well as in outdoor environment
